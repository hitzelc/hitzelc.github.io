Getting There

Car

The hotel is just off of I-95.

Plane

Flying into Logan Airport in Boston is likely to be easiest with the most flights. That'll be about a 1 hour drive to the hotel.

If you're looking for a smaller airport you can try Manchester, NH. That'll be about a 45 minute drive to the hotel.

Train

There is a train station in Exeter, NH. The Amtrak Downeaster stops there if you want to take a train out of Boston.

Things to Do

Portsmouth is a great town with lots of restaurants and shops. You'll be close to the coast, Prescott Park, Smuttynose, and other attractions.
