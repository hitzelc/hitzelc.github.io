Hello,

We are Chris and Sean of smallfarms.

We believe that localized production and short supply chains lead to greater community autonomy, smaller environmental footprints, and healthier living (both physically and spiritually).

We want to supply high quality, sustainable food products to local restuarants, bars, and cafes for reasonable prices.

We want people to fall back in love with the land around them. Our long term goals are big, but we're only getting started and we'll need help to become helpful ourselves!

As would-be farmers, step one for us is going to be figuring out what to grow, what local needs are, and what reasonable prices might be.
